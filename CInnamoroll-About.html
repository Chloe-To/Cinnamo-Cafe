About Cinnamoroll Café

Cinnamoroll is Cafe Cinnamoroll's official mascot. He is quite shy, but very friendly, and sometimes takes a nap on customers' laps. He can fly through the air by flapping his huge ears.

The first Cinnamoroll Café opened in Tokyo in 2016 but it wasn't permanent! Sanario has now decided to open a new Cinnamoroll Café in the heart of London Soho!

Come experience the most kawaii dining experience you will ever step foot upon and order from our extensive café menu!

Having a birthday party? Check out our events page to book the whole place out or a section of the café out for family and friends.
